CASE STUDY #1

Savings: $26,339.22 for the employer, $7,000 for the employee

Requested Surgery: Right Hip Arthroscopy with labral repair; 46 year old female in Utah

Coba Bundled Rate: $21,300 paid by her employer fund, zero out of pocket to the employee

Previous Surgery: Left Hip Arthroscopy with labral repair

Previous Cost: $47,639.22 paid by her “discount” network, plus $7,000 deductible paid by the employee

This patient was referred from a self-funded employer with about 2,000 employees. 2 years previous to this surgical need she had undergone a right hip arthroscopy with labral repair. Her result had been excellent, and she needed the other hip scoped and repaired. She requested the same surgeon and facility. The facility was already contracted with Coba Health. The surgeon was contacted and agreed to perform the surgery at our negotiated rate.

Result: The patient again had an excellent result. Her deductible was entirely waived by her employer.

CASE STUDY #2

Savings: $32,325 for the employer, $7000 for the employee

Requested Surgery: Left Total Knee Replacement, average “discount” network cost: $57,200

Patient: 48 year old male in New Jersey

Coba Bundled Rate: $24,875 paid by his employer fund, zero out of pocket to the employee

This patient contacted Coba Health through his HR Director and indicated that his need for pain pills had become an issue that was effecting his work quality and quality of life. He had suffered a football injury in high school many years earlier and had been experiencing increasing pain. Because of his work in the warehouse, he was no longer able to perform his work in a satisfactory manner.

Result: This patient had an excellent result and was able to return to work after 3 weeks. He experienced a high degree of pain relief and function after recovery. His deductible was entirely waived by his employer.

Requested Surgery: Revision spine fusion surgery, multi-level, average “discount network” cost: $97,200
